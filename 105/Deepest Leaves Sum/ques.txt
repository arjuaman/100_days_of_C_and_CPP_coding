Deepest Leaves Sum


Given the root of a binary tree, return the sum of values of its deepest leaves.

Ex: Given the following tree…

      2
     / \
    4   5, return 9 (4 and 5 are at the deepest level and sum to 9).
	
Ex: Given the following tree…

      1
       \
        2
         \
          3, return 3.
