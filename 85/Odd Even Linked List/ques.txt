Odd Even Linked List 


This question is asked by Facebook. 
Given a singly linked list, re-order and group its nodes in such a way that the nodes in odd positions come 
first and the nodes in even positions come last.

Ex: Given the reference to the following linked list...

4->7->5->6->3->2->1->NULL, return 4->5->3->1->7->6->2->NULL
Ex: Given the reference to the following linked list...

1->2->3->4->5->NULL, return 1->3->5->2->4->NULL
